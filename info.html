Hello! The name's Marek.

I like many things.

I like flailing wildly to rhythmic music in absolute euphoria. I like being still, feeling the weight of the universe pouring out of abstract sound. I like peering into utter humanity radiating through edited film. I like to learn, I like to comprehend, I like to eat up information and spit it back out again.

I appreciate compassion, creativity, clarity, curiousity, confidence, and courage. I appreciate artists of all disciplines. I don't like or appreciate oppressive and abusive power structures.

I am a software developer by trade, but I also am a musician, artist, writer, improviser, and poetic human being. I have goals and aspirations, but also take things day by day and just try things that inspire me. Overall, I'm just trying to live the best life that I can.
